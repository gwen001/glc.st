Courses

Welcome! I’m passionate about web security and ethical hacking - and I’m here to share that with you. Whether you’re just getting started or looking to level up your skills, my courses offer hands-on, real-world training to help you understand how websites are attacked and how to defend them. Let’s turn curiosity into capability.

Each course focuses on practical demonstrations, interactive exercises, and discussions on real-life scenarios to ensure the acquisition of key skills.

Each course can be done:

  • on site or remotely
  • in french or in english
  • indidividually or in groups
