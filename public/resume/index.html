About

After more than 15 years of web development, I finally decided to follow my dream: perform in the security industry. In 2015, after 3 months of hard work, I successfully passed the famous exam Penetration Testing with Kali Linux exam aka OSCP.

Then, security became my daily.
With more than 8 years of experience in bug bounty, I had the opportunity to study several aspects of this industry. I also performed many security audits with companies the last 5 years.

I am an active member of the community as I developped many tools to automate the basics of recon.
I created and maintain offsec.tools, a vast collection of security tools for bug bounty, pentest and red teaming. I now sell my services as an independent cybersecurity expert:
